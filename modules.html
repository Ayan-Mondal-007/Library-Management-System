LMS Modules

These modules help in the smooth functioning of Library Management Software

Cataloguing

It is the process of creating a list of all bibliographic items such as short description, list of subjects, author name, and the classification.

Membership Module

This module keeps track of all important information of students who have taken membership of the library.

Report Generator

It helps in generating various reports like status of issued books, total members, books returned etc.

Circulation

It is a central and highly visible function of the library, as it keeps the record of status of books in the library.

OPAC

Online Public Access Catalog, is an online database of materials kept in the library. It allows the search for an item of your choice in the library.

Acquisition

It is the department in the library which is responsible for the selection and purchase of material or resources in the library.

Periodicals

It helps in receiving periodicals issue and subscription details. It provides periodical accession number generation.

Reports

It can generate various types of reports like overdue reports, dynamic reports, resource status reports, transaction history, etc.

Database Management

This module helps in maintaining and organizing the database of the library. Keeping record of each and every item in the library.

System Administrator

This module helps in removing all the hassles of library management which lead to smooth functioning.

Transaction Management

Transaction is managing of all books, magazines, journals in the library for fast functioning of software.

Book Acquisition Control

It is a method of adding books to the stock of the library. The department selects vendors, negotiates, and arranges for standard orders and resources.
